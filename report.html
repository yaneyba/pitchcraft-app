Powered by Gemini AI

PitchCraft utilizes three specialized AI functions to provide comprehensive support. Each feature is designed to address a critical aspect of the pitching process, ensuring a well-rounded and effective final product.

Pitch Generation

Creates high-quality, persuasive pitches in various styles including Elevator, Investor, Social Media, and more. The AI adapts its tone and structure to perfectly match the chosen format.

Pitch Analysis

A simulated AI Venture Capitalist analyzes the generated pitch, providing structured feedback on its strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement, helping users refine their message.

Marketing Angles

Brainstorms catchy slogans and unique marketing strategies tailored to the user's app. This feature provides actionable ideas to kickstart a go-to-market plan.

The Founder's Dashboard

The user dashboard is the central hub for managing activity. It provides an at-a-glance view of key metrics and a complete history of all generated content, ensuring founders can track their progress and access past work easily.
